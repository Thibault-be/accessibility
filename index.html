Latest trip

Christ the Redeemer, a 98-foot-tall statue of Jesus, graces Rio de Janeiro's Corcovado mountain. Built in 1931 from reinforced concrete and soapstone, it symbolizes Christianity and is an iconic global attraction. With outstretched arms, it offers stunning views of Rio and its surroundings. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a testament to the city's cultural and religious significance.
